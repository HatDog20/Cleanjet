Our Car Wash Process

At CleanJet, we use a meticulous 7-step washing process to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible:

  • Pre-Wash Inspection: We document your vehicle's condition before we begin
  • Pre-Rinse: High-pressure rinse to remove loose dirt and debris
  • Foam Bath: pH-balanced soap application to lift dirt gently
  • Hand Wash: Two-bucket method with microfiber mitts
  • Wheel Cleaning: Specialized cleaners for wheels and tires
  • Final Rinse: Spot-free deionized water
  • Drying: Microfiber towels to prevent water spots
